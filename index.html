About us

JB Line, Inc. started its activities in October 2010, first as an unincorporated association, for the purpose of helping residents in New England by giving support and guidance in handling difficult or stressful activities of daily living.

JB Line, Inc. was incorporated in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts on May 9, 2012, as a non-profit corporation.

The mission of JB Line, Inc. is to provide outreach to persons who might otherwise not easily access social services through an anonymous and confidential 24/7 hotline, to identify services of our individual clients might require and to promote clients’ physical and mental well-being through the provision (either directly or through information services that allow clients easier access to available public services) of such services.

In addition to providing a 24/7 hotline, JB Line, Inc. provides case management services, counseling, visits to senior homes, support groups, and outreach meetings.
